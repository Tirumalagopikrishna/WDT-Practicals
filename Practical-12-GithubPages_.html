ADMISSIONS 2025–26

How to Apply

Joining Marwadi University is simple and transparent. Follow these steps to begin your academic journey.

1

Register Online

Create your profile at admissions.marwadiuniversity.ac.in

2

Submit Documents

Upload mark sheets, ID proof, and passport-size photograph

3

Entrance / Merit

Appear for MUSAT or provide JEE/GUJCET scores as applicable

4

Counselling & Offer

Attend counselling, select your program, and receive offer letter

5

Fee & Enrolment

Pay fees to confirm admission and collect your ID card
